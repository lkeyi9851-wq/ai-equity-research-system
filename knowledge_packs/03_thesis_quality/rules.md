# Thesis Quality Rules

A thesis is not a positive fact.

A thesis must pass:

```text
trend / event
-> industry mechanism
-> company exposure
-> competitive capture
-> financial line item
-> market expectation gap
-> valuation / rating impact
-> invalidation trigger
```

If it cannot pass this chain, classify it as:

```text
fact
observation
market framing
what to verify next
```

Do not place it in Core Thesis.

Compression rule:
- The final memo should say less, but every retained sentence must carry more decision value.
