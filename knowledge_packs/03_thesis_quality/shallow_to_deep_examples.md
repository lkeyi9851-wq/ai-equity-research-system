# Shallow To Deep Examples

Bad:

```text
AI demand is growing, so semiconductor companies should rise.
```

Better:

```text
AI demand supports a Buy thesis only if the company captures value at the bottleneck layer, such as advanced packaging, HBM, GPU architecture, foundry capacity, networking, or power management. The thesis must show whether this changes revenue, gross margin, FCF, or valuation beyond what current market expectations already price in.
```

Bad:

```text
Sany benefits from globalization.
```

Better:

```text
Sany's overseas growth matters only if overseas revenue remains both faster-growing and higher-margin than domestic revenue. If overseas GPM is materially above domestic GPM and overseas mix keeps rising without working-capital deterioration, group margin and FCF quality can improve, supporting re-rating. The thesis fails if overseas growth requires price cuts, credit expansion, or margin dilution.
```

Bad:

```text
Electrification is a growth driver.
```

Better:

```text
Electrification supports Sany's re-rating only if revenue contribution becomes material, margins are at least group-average, and adoption is not already reflected in estimates. If electrification remains narrative exposure without EPS or FCF impact, it belongs in Why Now or What to Verify Next, not Core Thesis.
```
