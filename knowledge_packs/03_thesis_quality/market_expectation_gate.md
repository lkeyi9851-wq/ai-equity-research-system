# Market Expectation Gate

Before Buy / Overweight, answer:

```text
What does the current valuation imply?
What do consensus estimates already assume?
What has the stock already priced?
What is underappreciated?
What evidence would force the market to revise?
```

If this section is missing, the memo is business analysis, not stock research.

Minimum pass:
- one current valuation implication
- one estimate or market expectation reference
- one underappreciated mechanism
- one revision catalyst
