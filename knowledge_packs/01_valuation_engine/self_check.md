# Valuation Self Check

Before publishing:

- Are all valuation inputs real, labeled, and dated?
- Did the memo recalculate key multiples?
- Are source multiples and recalculated multiples within 15%?
- If not, is confidence capped?
- Is each Bear/Base/Bull case tied to a real business or market mechanism?
- Does the final rating still hold under the conservative case?
- Is stale price clearly labeled?

Fail condition:
- If the valuation bridge contains invented cases, remove rating or downgrade confidence.
