# Triggers Self Check

Before publishing:

- Does every trigger have a metric?
- Does every trigger have a threshold or safety line?
- Does every trigger name the direction that hurts or helps the thesis?
- Is each trigger linked to a thesis condition?
- Is the rating action explicit?
- Are generic risk phrases removed?

Fail condition:
- If a sentence cannot be observed or measured, move it to risk framing or delete it.
