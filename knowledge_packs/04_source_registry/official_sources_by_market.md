# Official Sources By Market

Source hierarchy:

US:
- SEC EDGAR APIs for filings, submissions, and XBRL company facts.
- Company investor relations for earnings releases and presentations.

Hong Kong:
- HKEXnews for listed company announcements, reports, circulars, and regulatory filings.
- Company investor relations.

China A-share:
- Exchange/company announcements and annual reports.
- Company investor relations.
- AKShare / Tushare only as market-data or helper-data adapters, not official evidence.

Evidence rule:
- Official disclosure outranks data adapters and media.
- Analyst reports can support market-view references, not primary factual evidence.
