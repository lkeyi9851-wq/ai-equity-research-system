# Public Safety Policy

This repository is shared for educational and research purposes. It documents an
AI-assisted equity research workflow and should not be read as investment,
trading, legal, tax, or financial advice.

## Publishable

- Research workflow design.
- Agent role definitions.
- Source-handling and evidence-quality rules.
- Abstract knowledge packs.
- Synthetic examples.
- Historical examples rewritten only from public facts.
- Code, templates, schemas, and evaluation harnesses.

## Keep Private

- Real API keys, tokens, or local `.env` files.
- Personal research notes or live investment theses.
- Local input packages and generated outputs.
- Paid research, institutional reports, or excerpts from third-party articles.
- Proprietary data exports from terminals or paid data vendors.
- Case packs that reveal non-public reasoning, private source traces, or current
  investment intent.

## Public Example Rule

Public examples should teach the method without depending on a live stock call.
Prefer synthetic companies, anonymized historical patterns, or fully public
post-mortems where the outcome is already known.

## Disclaimer

The project may produce incomplete or incorrect analysis. Outputs should be
reviewed by a qualified human and should never be used as the sole basis for an
investment decision.
